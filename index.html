How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own drop tests or timed trials. Instead, we rely on decades of owner feedback from forums and review sites, plus published evaluations from respected tool reviewers. We look for patterns: which tapes develop a loose hook after a year, which blades start to curl, which locks jam up. A great tape has to earn its reputation through real-world use, not a lab score. Our shortlist comes from brands with proven track records for consistency and after-sale support. We also weigh how easy a tape is to read in low light, how comfortable it sits in a hand, and how well the belt clip stays on – because the little things matter when you’re up a ladder.
